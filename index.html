Our Philosophy

Plants first. People always.

Every blend begins with a relationship: with the land, with the families who tend it, and with the person it is made for.

  • Wildcrafted at the source

    Hand-harvested in the Andes and Amazon, where these plants have grown wild for generations.

  • Direct partnership

    We buy directly from Kichwa families. Fair, personal, and built on years of trust.

  • Whole botanicals

    Never extracts, never fillers. The whole leaf, bark, and root, exactly as nature made it.

  • Small batches

    Blended when you order and shipped fresh, so nothing sits on a shelf losing its life.

Ecuador Retreats

Come to the source.

Some medicine cannot be shipped. Each year, Raisa and Brian open their home in Ecuador to a small group of guests who want to meet the plants where they grow, in the soil and the rain that made them.

Days move slowly here. You will walk the forest with Kichwa healers who name each leaf as an old friend, sit in ceremony as their families have for generations, and drink guayusa brewed over a morning fire.

Between plant walks and waterfall visits, there is time to rest, ask questions, and simply be in the Amazon. Our next retreat gathers this August, and spaces are intentionally few.
